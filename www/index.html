I needed consistently sized logos for some of my recent projects. I wanted images that were:

  • svg format
  • consistently sized
  • consistent padding
  • transparent background
  • look good on a variety of light backgrounds
  • with type in the correct layout

I ended having to do a fair bit of work to get what I needed, and figured others might benefit as well.
